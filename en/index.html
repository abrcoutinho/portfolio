UOL

In order to broaden the business focus at UOL Affiliates, we conducted a strategic mapping to ensure that the product could remain competitive.

For twelve weeks, we facilitated meetings with stakeholders, generating a product discovery. At the end of the period, we had documented the vision, mission, and purpose of the business, the value proposition, a SWOT matrix highlighting strengths and opportunities, validation of personas and user journeys with qualitative research, and OKRs outlining goals.

I conceived a gamified incentive program to reward affiliates. I presented a theoretical study based on KPIs and a prototype, consisting of engagement models, a central mission with a type of steps representing sales performance, awards related to the size of the achievement, transactional and emotional motivators, and reward mechanics.
